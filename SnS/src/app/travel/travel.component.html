WANDERLUST

We had a plan to travel around the world but it turned out to be exhausting. Instead we have decided on taking shorter trips. With this idea we were able to enjoy every country we have visited and that is what we highly recommend! Taking instructor jobs while traveling helped us to stay afloat. Below we are presenting best places we have already visited. But you need to be careful: traveling is very addictive!

Iguazu, Brazil/Argentina

One of the biggest waterfall in the world.

The Philippines

Boracay for beaches, Siargao for good vibes. Definitely not for food!

Costarica

There is no better destination for wild life lovers.

Peru

Huarascan National Park? Canyon Colca? Or Machu Picchu?

Bolivia

Salar de Uyuni in Bolivia is the world's largest salt flat (over 10,000 square kilometers). During dry season we can see hexagonal salt formations on the surface. Following rain Uyuni transforms into the world's biggest mirror. Unforgettable view...

TO REWRITE Whole Wietnam is a beautiful country, but what we found the most interesting was the limestone formations in Ha Long Bay and in Tam Coc province. In Ha Long Bay we can see many islands on the water while Tam Coc has a very similar view but islands are between rice fields. Both of them are great.
